The Method

A structured, three-session performance preparation program. Designed to establish routines, and awareness required to perform consistently under pressure—while avoiding physical injury.

SESSION 1 — Identity (30 minutes)

Purpose: Establish a clear baseline.

The athlete leaves with:

  • Awareness of current preparation and recovery practices
  • Identification of gaps, inconsistencies, and common risk patterns
  • Clarity on potential blind spots
SESSION 2 — Guidance (45 minutes)

Purpose: Install a repeatable system.

The athlete leaves with:

Clear routines for:

  • Pre-training readiness
  • Post-training recovery
  • Mobility and durability

A simple system they can execute on training days, match days, and off-days.

SESSION 3 — Observation (60 minutes)

Purpose: Refine execution in real conditions.

The athlete leaves with:

  • Live feedback and corrections
  • Refined routines that hold up under fatigue and pressure
  • Confidence executing the system at home and on the pitch
