Section 3

The Final Third™ Method

A structured, three-session performance preparation program designed to establish the habits, routines, and awareness required to perform calmly and consistently under pressure—while reducing unnecessary physical breakdown.

Session 1
Identity
30 minutes · Baseline assessment

Establish a clear picture of how the athlete currently prepares, recovers, and approaches competition.

  • Awareness of current preparation and recovery habits
  • Identification of gaps, inconsistencies, and risk patterns
  • Clarity on potential blind spots under pressure
Session 2
Guidance
45 minutes · System installation

Install simple, repeatable routines that support both performance and durability.

  • Pre-training readiness routine
  • Post-training recovery routine
  • Mobility and durability routine
  • A simple system they can execute on training days, match days, and off-days
Session 3
Observation
60 minutes · Live refinement

Turn understanding into execution with real-time feedback in real environments.

  • Live feedback and practical corrections
  • Refined routines that hold up under fatigue and pressure
  • Confidence executing the system at home and on the pitch

Proof of concept: The Final Third™ grew out of real conversations, real matches, and a real journey to the college game.
